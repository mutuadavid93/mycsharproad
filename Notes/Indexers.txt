### Indexers
	Used in Sessions and SQL Server Datasource to read their values. You can 
		either use String or Intergral indexer to retrieve the value.

## Creating Indexers
	An indexer is created with this keyword and has both set and get accessors 
		as well as parameters. Just like a method.

## Indexers can be Overloaded based on the "Number" and "Type" of parameters.
