### Stack
	Observes the LIFO (Last In First Out).

	# We use Push() to add an item at the top of a stack.

	# Then Pop() to remove and return the value at the top of the stack

	# Peek() returns the item present at the top of the stack.

	# Contains() Looks whether the item exist in a stack.

NB: Instantiation of ALL the methods and the stack itself is;
	Syntax:

	ObjectType refvar = new ObjectType();
