### Delegates
	Its a type safe function pointer.

i.e A delegate points to a function and when you invoke this delegate the function 
	will be invoked.

	Gives you Flexibility.

NB: The signature of the delegate and that of the function which its pointing to must 
	be the "SAME".
		i.e the return type and Number and type of parameters.

NB: the method Must be "static" as well.
