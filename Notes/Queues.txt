### Queues:
	Observes FIFO (First In First Out).

	NB: We add items to a queue using Enqueue() method. The method adds items
		at the end of the queue.

	NB: Retrieve Item from a Queue, we use Dequeue(). Removes an item and 
		returns it from the beginning of the queue. Affects the queue capacity.

	NB: Retrieve and return Item from begining of Queue and not remove it, we use the 
		Peek() method.

	NB: Contains() => checks for existence of an object in the queue.
