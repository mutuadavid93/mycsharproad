### Types
	Means Classes, Enums, Structs, Interfaces and delegates. While properties 
	and methods are type members.

C# Access modifiers: 
	Private, Protected, Internal, Protected Internal and Public.

	NB: Type members can have all the access modifiers (5) whereas Types
		can only have two (2 i.e internal, public) of the five (5) modifiers.

Regious help collapse pieces of C# code
	Syntax; 
		#region RegionName
			//content
		#endregion

	Alternative: Expand and collapse regions using;
